My Story: From a Village to Tech Dreams

Where I Come From

I grew up in a small village where we didn't have many things people in cities take for granted. We didn't have good internet, manytimes the electricity would go out, and even getting clean water was hard. But these challenges didn't stop me from being curious about how things work, especially when it came to technology.

My First Steps into Tech

Even though I didn't have a computer, I was always trying to figure things out. Here are some things I did:

  • I tried to make my dad's old phone faster by rooting it,so I could play games on it. It didn't work, but I learned a lot about how phones work.
  • When I was 10, I really wanted a glue gun but couldn't buy one. So I made my own using parts from other things around the house.
  • I once tried to heat water using electricity. It caused a big explosion and filled the house with smoke! My parents weren't happy .The incident was so dangerous that even the electrician said someone must have died of it.
  • I made other things too to keep working with electronics which I couldn't afford, like a tool to cut foam, my own soldering iron, an alarm system using a small computer called Arduino and worked on human following robot (but couldn't complete due to lack of funds.).

    • I started learning to code on my phone. The internet in my village was very slow, but I would walk around trying to find spots with better signal. I learned about something called Machine Learning from a course I got after winning a competition. That's how I got into AI and ML.Then I got into LLM's, Ethical Hacking, Hardware and AR development.
