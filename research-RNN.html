RNN & LSTM Networks

What are RNN and LSTM?

RNN stands for Recurrent Neural Networks.
LSTM stands for Long Short-Term Memory.

How RNN Works

RNNs are a kind of neural network, but with a loop, where info from one neuron 
is passed on to the next neuron.

It can be thought of as multiple copies of the same network, with message 
passing to a successor.

RNNs are capable of learning short-term dependencies.

How LSTM Works

LSTMs are a special kind of RNN with the capability of learning long-term 
dependencies.

Their default behavior is to remember information for long periods of time.
